DashAI: An interactive platform for AI

Still a work in progress, DashAI is aimed to be an open source interactive platform to facilitate the experimentation of ML models for various tasks on tabular data, NLP and computer vision. Some of the main features of the software will be:

Experimentation

Load your datasets and train different models for the task you want to work on.

DashAI allows you to set the parameters of your model and configure every step of the ML workflow with a high level of detail.

Easy visualization of your experiments results

Our philosophy is Open Source

Our software has an MIT license and is intended to invite from practitioners to researchers to be part of the DashAI community
